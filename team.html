Team

Our leaders and facilitators

Benedict Tokpa Danuweli
National Coordinator

Benedict Tokpa Danuweli is a dynamic, results-driven professional dedicated to driving excellence in business and organizational performance. With over a decade of diverse experience spanning the public, private and non-profit sectors, he leads transformational leadership development programs for EQUIP Leadership Liberia. Benedict is committed to the ambitious goal of training and certifying 125,000 leaders in Liberia by 2026.

Lawrence V. C. Dennis, Jr
Programs Coordinator

Mr. Lawrence V. C. Dennis, Jr. is a driver of positive change and champions the vision for transformational leadership. His impact spans public and private sectors, contributing to policy development and strategic planning while promoting fiscal discipline and resource mobilisation. At EQUIP, he coordinates and administers roundtable activities to develop intentional change agents, and his voluntary initiatives support community development at the national level.

Lamie A. Sallay
Lead Facilitator

Mr. Lamie A. Sallay facilitates the Beyond Success program and provides administrative leadership to the EQUIP team. He is CEO of the University of Liberia Credit Union (ULCU), a peacebuilder and advocate for positive change, and a faculty member in Public Administration at the University of Liberia. He holds a Bachelor and Master of Public Administration and is a law student at the Louis Arthur Grimes School of Law.

Michael Koilor
Coordinator, Montserrado

Michael Koilor is dedicated to serving his community. Married with two children, he holds a BBA in Accounting and an advanced certificate in computer studies. He leads youth-focused programmes that empower young people to become catalysts for positive change, with a vision to free youth from the influence of illicit drugs and harmful substances.
