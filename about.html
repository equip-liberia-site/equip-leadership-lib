About Equip Leadership Liberia

Who we are

Equip Leadership Liberia is a nonprofit subsidiary of Equip Leadership Global (John C. Maxwell). Since 2018 we've trained and mobilized transformational leaders across multiple sectors — reaching over 16,000 Liberians.

Vision

To be the driver of transformational leadership development in Liberia and surrounding regions by grooming leaders to transform their nations.

Mission

We develop servant leaders through licensed John Maxwell programs and targeted community engagement — empowering individuals to influence their circles and communities.

Impact & Approach

Our approach uses facilitator-led roundtables, youth clubs, institutional trainings and partnerships with NGOs to ensure sustainable results. We focus on measurable outcomes, mentorship and local ownership.

  • Facilitator-led roundtables
  • Youth leadership clubs (Beyond Success Club)
  • Partnerships with schools and NGOs
Meet the team

History & Reach

Founded on the principles of John C. Maxwell’s leadership model, Equip Leadership Liberia started operations in 2018 and has been active across counties with community-focused programs and facilitator development.
