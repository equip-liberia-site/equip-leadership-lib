About Equip Leadership Liberia

Who we are

Equip Leadership Liberia is a non-profit organisation dedicated to developing servant leaders across Liberia. Launched in 2018 as a local partner of Equip Leadership Global, we deliver licensed John C. Maxwell leadership programmes, facilitator training and community partnerships that create lasting, measurable impact.

Our Vision

To be the leading force for transformational leadership in Liberia and the wider region — inspiring leaders who build healthy institutions and thriving communities.

Our Mission

We equip people with practical leadership skills, mentorship and resources so they can lead with character, influence others for good, and multiply positive change in their communities.

Our Approach & Programs

We combine proven leadership curricula with local facilitation and partnership models:

  • Facilitator training: certify local trainers to deliver high-quality sessions.
  • Beyond Success Clubs: youth-focused small groups that build values, confidence and practical skills.
  • Institutional trainings: tailored programmes for schools, NGOs and businesses.
